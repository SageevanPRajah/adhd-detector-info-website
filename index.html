Problem Statement

ADHD Diagnosis is Broken

Early identification of ADHD in children is difficult because diagnosis mainly relies on long clinical observations by specialists. Traditional methods are time-consuming, subjective, and expensive, causing many children to remain undiagnosed or diagnosed late.

Research Gap

Existing ADHD detection methods rely on time-consuming manual clinical observations and rarely integrate multiple behavioral indicators into a single automated system.

Doctors & Child Psychologists who need efficient screening tools.

👨‍👩‍👧

Main Objective

AI-based early ADHD screening analyzing multiple behavioral indicators.

Key Features

Four Behavioral Analysis Modules

Our platform integrates four distinct AI-powered modules, each analyzing a different behavioral indicator associated with ADHD.

👁️

Eye Tracking Analysis

Detects visual attention patterns and gaze fixation abnormalities using computer vision to identify ADHD-related visual inattention.

🧍

Posture & Hyperactivity Detection

Monitors body movement and posture using pose estimation to detect hyperactivity levels during tasks.

🎙️

Speech Pattern Analysis

Analyzes voice patterns, interruptions, and speech rhythm to identify language-related ADHD indicators.

✏️

Handwriting Pattern Analysis

Examines digital handwriting behavior including pressure, speed, and consistency to detect fine motor irregularities.
